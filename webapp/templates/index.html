Welcome to the Tarot!

Having trouble making a decision? Worried about the future? Concerned that secret forces are controlling your destiny? Then it's time to consult the Tarot..

Think about your question and chose one or three cards:

draw one random card
(the answer to your question)

draw three random cards
(the past, the present and future of the matter)

list all the cards

This is a simple web application written in Python to draw one or more randomly selected cards from a dictionary (hash) of 72 Tarot card description and image references.

Please feel free to fork or re-use, no license restrictions (the tarot images are my own captures of Jean Noblet's version of the Marseille deck).
