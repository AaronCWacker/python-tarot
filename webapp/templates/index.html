My name is Jeremy Tarling and I work as a data specialist at the BBC. I've been fascinated by the tarot since childhood when my aunty first introduced me to the cards.

This little website is a simple application written in Python to draw one or more randomly selected tarot cards from a dictionary of 72 descriptions and image references.

The tarot images are my own captures of Jean-Claude Flornoy's wonderful restoration of Jean Noblet's deck, in the Marseille tradition.

The source code to this app is here - please feel free to fork or re-use, there are no license restrictions.
